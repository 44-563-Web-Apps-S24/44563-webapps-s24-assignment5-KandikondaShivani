KIWI JUICE

Kiwi juice, rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants, supports immune function and combats oxidative stress, potentially lowering chronic disease risks. Its high dietary fiber content promotes digestion and prevents constipation. The enzyme actinidin aids in protein digestion, enhancing nutrient absorption. Regular consumption may improve skin health, thanks to Vitamin E and collagen synthesis support. Additionally, kiwi juice's potassium content helps in managing blood pressure levels, contributing to cardiovascular health.
